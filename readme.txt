Easy Text-to-Speech is a powerful, user-friendly WordPress plugin designed to transform your textual content into high-quality synthesized speech.

== Description ==

Easy Text-to-Speech effortlessly transforms your website's written content into spoken audio, making your site more accessible, engaging, and inclusive. This WordPress plugin integrates cutting-edge text-to-speech technology from top-tier services such as Amazon Polly, OpenAI, and ElevenLabs.

Whether you're looking to reach a broader audience, including those with visual impairments, or simply enhance the user experience with audio, this plugin provides an easy solution.

= Key Features =

- **Diverse Technology Options:** Choose between Amazon Polly, OpenAI, and ElevenLabs for access to industry-leading text-to-speech technology, allowing you to select the best voice for your content.
- **Effortless Integration:** Works seamlessly with both WordPress's Classic and Block Editors, making it simple to add audio to your posts, pages, and articles.
- **Broad Accessibility:** Make your content accessible to those with visual impairments or those who prefer auditory learning experiences, expanding your audience.
- **Intuitive User Interface:** A clean, user-friendly interface that allows for a quick and straightforward setup, no technical expertise required.
- **Customizable Audio Experience:** Choose from multiple voice options and adjust speech settings to match your website's tone and style.

With Easy Text-to-Speech, you can make your content more accessible and engaging, offering a spoken version of your text and providing a richer experience for all visitors.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `/easy-text-to-speech` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin via the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the settings and start converting text to speech on your site!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does it work with the classic editor? =
Yes, Easy Text-to-Speech is fully compatible with both Classic and Block editors.

= Can I use it with my custom post types? =
Absolutely! You can use this plugin with any custom post types on your site.

= Where do I get my Amazon Polly API key? =
Follow the instructions on our [installation guide](https://handyplugins.co/docs/easy-text-to-speech-installation-configuration/) to get your Amazon Polly API key.
