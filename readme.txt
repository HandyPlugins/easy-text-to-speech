Easy Text-to-Speech is a powerful, user-friendly WordPress plugin designed to transform your textual content into high-quality synthesized speech.

== Description ==

Easy Text-to-Speech transforms your website's text into high-quality spoken audio, opening up a new dimension of accessibility and user engagement.
This WordPress plugin integrates cutting-edge text-to-speech technology from Amazon Polly, OpenAI, and ElevenLabs, making it effortless to provide an audio version of your content.

= Key Features =

- __Diverse Technology Options:__ Select from Amazon Polly, OpenAI, and ElevenLabs to access the best in speech synthesis.
- __Effortless Integration:__ Designed to work smoothly with both WordPress's Classic and Block Editors, enabling audio conversion for blog posts, articles, and pages effortlessly.
- __Broad Accessibility:__ Expand your audience by making your content easily accessible to people with visual impairments or those who prefer auditory learning.
- __Intuitive User Interface:__ A clean, straightforward interface allows quick setup.

With Easy Text-to-Speech, enrich your site's experience by providing a spoken version of your content, making your website more inclusive and engaging for all visitors.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the entire `/easy-text-to-speech` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate Easy Text-to-Speech through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Enjoy!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

Yes, it works with both classic and block editors.
